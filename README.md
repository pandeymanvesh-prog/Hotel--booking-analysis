# Hotel Booking Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to identify booking patterns, cancellation trends, customer behavior, and business opportunities. The objective is to derive actionable insights that can help hotels improve occupancy rates, reduce cancellations, and increase profitability.

---

## Business Problem

Hotel cancellations create revenue loss, inefficient room utilization, and forecasting challenges. This analysis aims to understand the key factors influencing booking behavior and provide data-driven recommendations for business improvement.

---

## Dataset Information

- Total Records: 30,000
- Domain: Hospitality Analytics
- Data Includes:
  - Booking Status
  - Booking Channel
  - Room Type
  - Hotel Rating
  - Customer Booking Information

---

## Objectives

- Analyze booking trends and customer behavior.
- Identify cancellation patterns.
- Evaluate booking channels.
- Compare room-type performance.
- Assess business risks and opportunities.
- Provide actionable recommendations.

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Data Analysis Techniques
- PowerPoint
- Business Analytics

---

## Key Findings

### Booking Status Analysis
- Approximately 72% of bookings were confirmed.
- Cancellation rate was around 20%.
- Cancellations significantly impact occupancy planning and revenue forecasting.

### Channel Analysis
- Travel Agent bookings showed the highest cancellation rate.
- Direct bookings were comparatively more reliable.
- Hotels can benefit from increasing direct booking share.

### Room Type Analysis
- Standard Rooms experienced the highest cancellation rates.
- Deluxe and Suite bookings showed stronger customer commitment.

### Hotel Rating Analysis
- 4-Star Hotels generated the highest booking volume.
- This segment represents the strongest growth opportunity.

---

## Root Cause Analysis

Major factors contributing to cancellations:

- Price sensitivity
- Indirect booking channels
- Last-minute travel plan changes
- Flexible cancellation policies
- Competitive pricing across platforms

---

## Business Impact

- Revenue loss
- Unused room inventory
- Reduced forecasting accuracy
- Operational inefficiencies
- Lower profitability

---

## KPI Metrics Analyzed

- Cancellation Rate
- Occupancy Rate
- Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR)
- Direct Booking Share
- Customer Retention Rate

---

## Recommendations

1. Increase direct booking incentives.
2. Introduce partial prepayment or deposit policies.
3. Implement loyalty and referral programs.
4. Use dynamic pricing strategies.
5. Monitor channel-wise cancellation performance.
6. Build real-time KPI dashboards for decision-making.

---

## Expected Outcomes

- 10–15% reduction in cancellations
- Increased direct bookings
- Improved occupancy planning
- Better customer retention
- Higher profitability

---

## Future Scope

- Predictive cancellation modeling
- Revenue forecasting
- Customer segmentation
- Seasonal trend analysis
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis

---

## Conclusion

The analysis demonstrates that booking channels and room categories significantly influence cancellation behavior. By adopting data-driven strategies focused on customer retention, direct bookings, and pricing optimization, hotels can improve operational efficiency and achieve sustainable business growth.
